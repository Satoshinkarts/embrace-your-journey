# 🏍️ Project Habal Moto — Platform Summary

## The Problem

In Iloilo City, motorcycle ride-hailing ("habal-habal") is coordinated through informal Facebook groups and Messenger threads. This creates:

- **Zero accountability** — no ride logs, no ratings, no verification
- **Safety risks** — unvetted riders, no tracking, no dispute resolution
- **Inefficiency** — manual dispatch, no fare standardization, no analytics

---

## The Solution

**Habal** is a managed motorcycle ride marketplace that brings structure, accountability, and safety to community-based rider networks — without disrupting the existing social fabric.

---

## How It Works

### For Customers
- Open the app → GPS auto-locates pickup
- Enter destination or tap a recent/frequent location to rebook
- See fare upfront (₱40 base + ₱10/km + zone premium)
- Track rider in real-time with live ETA
- Rate and review after every ride

### For Riders
- Accept ride requests from nearby customers
- Navigate with in-app maps
- Track earnings and trip history
- Build reputation through ratings and rankings

### For Operators (Network Owners)
- Onboard informal rider communities as licensed networks
- Manage members, view analytics, enforce governance
- Issue strikes and monitor KPI/SLA compliance
- Control wallet balances and rider payouts

### For Admins
- Full platform oversight — users, zones, networks, rides
- Zone-based dispatch allocation with premium pricing
- Wallet management and financial controls
- Real-time monitoring of all active rides

---

## Business Model

| Revenue Stream | Details |
|----------------|---------|
| **Operator License** | ₱35,000/month per network (includes 30 verified rider seats) |
| **Zone Dispatch Rights** | Premium fees per zone assigned to networks |
| **Platform Fees** | Transaction-based fees on ride completions |

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **Network-First Model** — Onboards existing rider communities as partners, not competitors
2. **Governance Built-In** — Strike system, KPI tracking, wallet penalties
3. **Zone-Based Dispatch** — Geographic rights management for fair distribution
4. **Full Accountability** — Every ride logged, rated, and auditable
5. **Mobile-First** — Customer & Rider apps optimized for smartphone use
